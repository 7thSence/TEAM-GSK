How to Create PMS

When to Create PMS

  • When preventive maintenance is due based on equipment history.
  • For scheduled service of critical machinery to avoid downtime.
  • When it aligns with the maintenance schedule for assets.

When Not to Create PMS

  • If the equipment has no record of previous issues requiring maintenance.
  • When creating PMS would result in redundant service orders.

How to Create PMS

  • Log into the service order management system.
  • Navigate to the " Preventive Maintenance" section.
  • Select the equipment or asset requiring PMS from the list.
  • Input the necessary details such as the maintenance type, date, and priority level.
  • Attach any relevant documents or maintenance history records.
  • Assign the task to the appropriate technician or team.
  • Save and confirm the PMS creation.
  • Notify the team or individual responsible for executing the maintenance task.
