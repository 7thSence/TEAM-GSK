Action

  • Within TAT:

    Thank the customer for patience and confirm resolution.

  • Post TAT:

    Escalate to the team for review.

Sub-Scenario: Parts Not Available

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Inform the customer of delay due to parts availability.

  • Post TAT:

    Escalate and provide new TAT.

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Confirm resolution with the customer.

  • Post TAT:

    Send follow-up survey for feedback.

Sub-Scenario: Issue Escalated

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Provide detailed case notes to management.

  • Post TAT:

    Update the customer with status.

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Document the reason for hold.

  • Post TAT:

    Contact the customer to resume service.

Status: Closed

Sub-Scenario: Case Successfully Closed

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Archive case details and notify the customer.

  • Post TAT:

    Send closure report to management.

Status: Awaiting Feedback

Sub-Scenario: Customer Follow-Up Required

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Contact the customer for additional details.

  • Post TAT:

    Send a follow-up email requesting feedback.

Status: Under Review

Sub-Scenario: Pending QA Approval

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Provide QA team with supporting documents.

  • Post TAT:

    Follow up with QA for expedited approval.

Status: In Progress

Action

  • Within TAT:

    Inform the customer of the technician details.

  • Post TAT:

    Reschedule the technician visit and notify the customer.
