How to Create PMS

When to Create PMS

  • When customer asks for a general service of their AC/Water purifier.
  • There's no other repair problem in AC
  • When customer agrees for Jet chagers, create the same

When Not to Create PMS

  • When customer also have repair problem (sound, not cooling etc)
  • When auto PMS is already generated

PMS FOFO Digital Service Order Process (SAP CRM)

  • Sold to party: Click on "Sold to party" field and then enter the Account 2 series ID
  • Contact: Click on "Contact" field and also input the Account 2 series ID
  • Category: Select Jet/wet and dry from the dropdown list
  • Reference objects: Again choose Jet/wet and dry as your reference objects
  • Reference objects (SO details): Enter the relevant Object ID
  • Items - Product ID: Input a product from the 6 series, then specify quantity, set unit to 1, and press Enter
  • Deferment date & Appointment end date: Set both to be identical, aligned with the customer’s requested date
  • Notes as per VOC: Add any notes provided by the Voice of Customer (VOC) for additional clarity
  • Save: Finally, click on Save to confirm and generate the PMS request
